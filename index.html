My Philosophy

My work strongly resonates with 4 core values - a framework I call VENT.

V

Vitality

Etymology: Latin vitalis - of life, vital

Life force in motion. Systems that breathe, pulse, and evolve. My generative work channels the organic unpredictability of living systems - embracing emergence over control, letting algorithms find their own rhythm.

E

Expression

Etymology: Latin expressare - to press out, represent

Authenticity made visible. Code as language, visuals as voice. Each piece is an act of pressing internal visions outward - transforming abstract thought into tangible, experiential reality without compromise.

N

Novelty

Etymology: Latin novus - new, fresh, unusual

The pursuit of unexplored terrain. Generative art is infinite possibility space - I seek the unfamiliar, the unexpectedly beautiful, the patterns that shouldn't exist but do. Innovation over iteration.

T

Truth

Etymology: Old English trÄ“owth - faithfulness, veracity

Honest computation. No deception, no artifice - only the raw logic of mathematics made beautiful. My work reveals the underlying structures of reality, making the invisible visible through algorithmic honesty.

Through TouchDesigner, I build systems that embody these principles - creating autonomous visual experiences that serve personal sovereignty and point toward utopian futures built on authentic expression.
